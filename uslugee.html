Services

 
Our office primarily provides services in the area of corporate, tax and civil law.



Corporate law

Corporate law /business law/ covers the area of statutory rights of companies and business contracts. We provide for our clients services ranging from the establishment of companies (or take over of the existing ones) and advisory services related to everyday operations of companies. The primary goal of our advisory role is to secure a full legality of operations with a maximal degree of legal protection for the interests of our clients. Our work is based on the principle that an advantage should be given to a preventive counselling in order to avoid disputes or potential legal or financial risks.

In the area of acquisitions and investments, we conduct a complete or partial legal due diligence whereby the risk of a certain business transaction is assessed. 

Many renowned corporations worldwide have recognized our commitment to corporate law. 

Reference list of our clients available upon request.


 
Tax law

We provide services in the area of tax law to ensure a safe and efficient business operations of our clients, either in form of prior tax counselling or the representation in tax proceedings. Our priority is the knowledge of the area of direct and indirect taxes, international accounting standards (IAS) and other regulations related to this field. The importance of this area of law for everyday operations of our clients is immense and we pay particular attention to a permanent education in this field.


 
Civil law

In a classical science of law the area of civil law includes also the law on obligations and proprietary law, in addition to corporate law. Everyday commercial practice implies numerous complex contractual relations whose establishment, scope and termination, are regulated by the law on obligations. The area of contractual liability for losses is of no less importance.
